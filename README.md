## About

I design and ship complete AI systems — not isolated notebooks. Over the past two years I've taken a medical imaging model from architecture to **98.53% validated accuracy**, and independently shipped a production booking platform now live for a UAE client. ### Brain Tumor Detection & Segmentation
![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Deployed-CD1C18?style=flat-square)

Dual-encoder VGG16 + ResNet18 UNet++ for simultaneous 4-class tumor classification and pixel-level MRI segmentation.

Patient-level `GroupShuffleSplit` prevents data leakage. CLAHE preprocessing + augmentation suite. Deployed on Hugging Face Spaces via Docker.
